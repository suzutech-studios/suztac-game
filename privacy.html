Privacy Policy for SuzTac

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how SuzTac handles information. By using our application, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Don't Collect

We believe in privacy by design. SuzTac is a completely self-contained, client-side application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any personal or sensitive information from you or your device. This includes:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information.
  • Your location data.
  • Usage analytics, game statistics, or crash reports.
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or browser fingerprints.
  • Any data you generate while playing the game. All game state is stored locally on your device during your session and is discarded when you close the page.

2. How Information is Used

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use it for any purpose. We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties because we do not have any data to share, sell, or rent.

3. Third-Party Services

SuzTac uses the Tailwind CSS library, which is loaded from a third-party Content Delivery Network (CDN). This is purely for styling the application's user interface. We do not control the privacy practices of this CDN, but its purpose is solely to deliver the stylesheet efficiently.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application is designed for a general audience and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: privacy@suztac.example.com
